Semantic Elements

A semantic element clearly describes its meaning to both the browser and the developer. Examples of non-semantic elements: "div" and "span" - Tells nothing about its content. Examples of semantic elements: "form", "table", and "article" - Clearly defines its content. More; "aside", "details", "mark"...
